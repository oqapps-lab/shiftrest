## Root-cause clusters

- **3 of 4 fixed bugs are "fallback drift"** (B1, B3, B5): three different surfaces had three different fallback chains (or no fallback wiring) over the same source of truth (auth + onboarding + mock). After fixes, Home greeting and Profile both pull through the same priority order; transition modal pulls from `useActiveTransitionPlan` with mock fallback.
- **B7 + B8 cluster** is "freshness of `useStreak`": both about query lifetime. Adding a `loading` flag exposed to consumers + Realtime subscription would close both at once.
